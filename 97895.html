Apple Pie Granola

Ingredienser (1 sats)

  • 1 cup hazelnuts
  • 1/2 cup pecans
  • 1 1/2 cup GF rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup sunflower seeds
  • 3 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • 4 tbsp maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup dried apples

Gor sa har

  1. Preheat oven to 140°C (280°F). In a food processor chop roughly whole hazelnuts and pecans. In a mixing bowl, add all dry ingredients (except apples) and stir. Add maple syrup and oil and mix throughly. Pour the mixture evenly onto a parchment lined baking sheet. Bake for 10 minutes, then stir and spread back to an even layer. Return to the oven for 10 more minutes. Cut dried apples in small pieces. Let it cool then combine with apple slices. Enjoy!
