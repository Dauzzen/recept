Chocolate Chip Muffins

Ingredienser (10 stycken)

  • 200g almond flour
  • 30g coconut flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 medium ripe bananas
  • 60g agave syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs (or flax eggs)
  • 50g coconut oil, melted
  • 100g extra dark chocolate chips

Gor sa har

  1. Preheat the oven to 170 °C (350°F).
  2. In a large bowl, place the flour blend, baking powder, salt, and whisk to combine. In a small cup, place the baking soda, combine with the lemon juice and add it to the flour blend.
  3. In another bowl, mash the bananas to form a puree. Add the agave syrup and vanilla and stir. Add in the eggs and oil and stir until well combined. Add in the dry mixture and stir with a wooden spoon. Finally fold in the chocolate chips and stir gently.
  4. Spoon the batter into a muffin liners and sprinkle on additional chocolate chips. Bake for 20 minutes. Let it cool. Enjoy!
