Golden Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredienser (10 stycken)

  • 3/4 cup (190ml) gluten free rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) almond flour flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt salt
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup (125ml) coconut sugar
  • 1 small egg (or flax egg)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Gor sa har

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350 °F). In a medium bowl, whisk together the oats, almond flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt. In another bowl, whisk peanut butter, coconut oil and sugar until creamy. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined. On low speed, add in the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  2. Scoop the cookies into round balls and place them on a parchment lined baking sheet leaving 5cm in between cookies. Bake for 10-12 minutes and remove when edges begin to turn a golden brown. Let cool on the baking sheet for about 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack and cool completely. Enjoy!
